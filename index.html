At Cherie, photography is a considered blend of artistry, emotion, and refined aesthetics. Founded by Morya Ghorpade, the studio is guided by an editorial sensibility where every frame is composed with intention, elegance, and a deep appreciation for detail. Each image is crafted to feel timeless, intimate, and quietly luxurious.

We are privileged to collaborate with discerning clients who value authenticity and a thoughtful visual narrative. With gratitude at the core of our approach, every project is treated as a bespoke, collaborative experience—personal, respectful, and meticulously tailored. Our aim is to create imagery that not only captivates, but endures with meaning and grace.
